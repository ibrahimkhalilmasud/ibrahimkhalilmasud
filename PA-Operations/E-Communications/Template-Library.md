# 💬 Communication Templates Library

> Ready-to-use templates for WhatsApp messages and emails.  
> All templates use a **direct, professional tone** as preferred by Boss.  
> Replace `{placeholders}` with actual information before sending.

---

## Template 1 — Daily Morning Brief (WhatsApp)

```
Good morning Sir. Here is your update for {DATE} ({DAY}, {CITY} Time).

📍 Current Location: {CITY}, {COUNTRY}
✈️ Next Flight: {FLIGHT_NO} | {ROUTE} | {DEPARTURE_DATE} at {DEPARTURE_TIME_LOCAL}
🏨 Hotel: {HOTEL_NAME} | Check-out: {CHECKOUT_DATE}
📋 Today's Schedule:
  • {TASK_OR_MEETING_1}
  • {TASK_OR_MEETING_2}
💳 Upcoming Payments: {PAYMENT_DETAILS or "None today"}
🔔 Reminders: {ALERTS or "No urgent reminders"}

Best regards,
{PA_NAME}
```
