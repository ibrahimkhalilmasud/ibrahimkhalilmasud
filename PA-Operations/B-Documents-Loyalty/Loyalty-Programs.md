## 🎯 Points Strategy

### How to Maximise Points

1. **Always quote loyalty number at booking** — never miss earning miles
2. **Credit all flights to the highest-value program** — compare earn rates before booking
3. **Use co-branded credit card** for partner point boosts
4. **Double-dip where possible** — earn both airline miles + hotel points
5. **Monitor upgrade availability** with accumulated miles (Qatar Silver gets priority waitlisting)

### Avios / BA Executive Club
- Avios can be used across the **IAG group**: BA, Iberia, Vueling, Aer Lingus
- Great for BCN–LHR–BCN routes (Vueling)
- Avios **do not expire** as long as account is active (1 transaction per 36 months)

### KrisFlyer (Singapore Airlines)
- Miles expire **3 years after date of earning**
- Elite miles earned on SQ can contribute to tier renewal

### Qatar Privilege Club (Silver)
- Boss holds **Silver** status
- Silver benefits: extra baggage, priority check-in, lounge access on select routes
- Tier renewal: check Qpoints / Tier Miles requirement annually
