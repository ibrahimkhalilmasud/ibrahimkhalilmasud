## 💡 PA Quick Tips — What Earns Boss's Trust

Based on working with Boss:

1. ✅ **Be extremely detailed** — no missing information
2. ✅ **Update proactively** — don't wait to be asked
3. ✅ **Report mistakes immediately, then fix** — he values honesty
4. ✅ **Send detailed travel updates** — especially important as he is a senior traveller (67)
5. ✅ **Never miss payments or booking deadlines**
6. ✅ **Professional, direct tone** — no fluff, no informality
7. ✅ **Keep both physical and digital records**
8. ✅ **Anticipate needs** — if a flight is booked, also check seats, meals, and lounge access
