## 🔍 Cruise Deal Monitoring

Boss enjoys receiving updates about cruise deals. Monitor the following:

| Source | URL | Check Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| MSC Cruises | https://www.msccruises.com | Monthly |
| Royal Caribbean | https://www.royalcaribbean.com | Monthly |
| Cunard | https://www.cunard.com | Monthly |
| Cruise.com | https://www.cruise.com | Monthly |
| LastMinuteCruises | https://www.lastminutecruises.com | Weekly (for deals) |

**Deal Alert Criteria:**
- Caribbean / Mediterranean routes
- Minimum balcony cabin
- Duration: 7–14 nights preferred
- Significant discount vs. standard rate (>20%)
- Senior discount availability (Boss: 67, Wife: 58)

---

*💡 When sharing cruise deals: include ship name, route, dates, cabin type, price per person, and booking link.*
