## ✅ Meeting Confirmation Process

Before every meeting, send Boss this WhatsApp confirmation (24 hours before):

> *"Good morning Sir. Confirming your meeting tomorrow, [DATE] at [TIME LOCAL TIME] with [COUNTERPARTY] at [LOCATION]. Please let me know if there are any changes."*

After the meeting:
1. Update Outcome field
2. Log any Follow-up Actions with deadlines
3. Set reminders for follow-up tasks

## 📈 Business Performance Updates

Boss expects regular updates on:
- Company performance summaries
- Project status updates
- Investment updates (especially Bali, Indonesia)
- Deal progress

Format for business update (WhatsApp):

> *"Good morning Sir. Here is your business update for [DATE].*
> *📊 [Company/Project Name]: [Key metric or update]*
> *📍 Bali Property: [Update if any]*
> *📋 Pending Actions: [List]*
> *Please advise if any action is required."*
