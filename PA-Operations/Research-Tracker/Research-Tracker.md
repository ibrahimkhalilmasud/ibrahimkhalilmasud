## 🔍 How to Research Flights

### Step 1 — Google Flights
1. Go to [flights.google.com](https://flights.google.com)
2. Enter route and dates — use "Flexible dates" for ±3 days
3. Filter by preferred airlines (Qatar, SQ, EK, MH, BA, Vueling)
4. Note aircraft type (check SeatGuru for seat map)
5. Note loyalty miles earnable (check airline's miles calculator)

### Step 2 — Skyscanner
1. Go to [skyscanner.com](https://skyscanner.com)
2. Search same route — compare prices with Google Flights
3. Check "Whole month" view for cheapest dates
4. Note any significant price difference

### Step 3 — Direct Airline Website
1. Check airline's direct website — sometimes cheaper than OTAs
2. Confirm seat map and aircraft type on seat map tools (e.g. [seatguru.com](https://seatguru.com))
3. Use loyalty program sign-in for member fares

### Step 4 — Prepare Comparison Table
Present to Boss in this format:

```
Good morning Sir. Here are the best options for {ROUTE} on {DATES}:

Option 1: {AIRLINE} — {FLIGHT_NO}
  Class: {CLASS} | Aircraft: {AIRCRAFT}
  Dep: {TIME_LOCAL} | Arr: {TIME_LOCAL}
  Price: {AMOUNT} {CURRENCY} ({MYR_EQUIV} MYR)
  Miles Earnable: ~{MILES} {PROGRAM}
  Book: {LINK}

Option 2: {AIRLINE} — {FLIGHT_NO}
  Class: {CLASS} | Aircraft: {AIRCRAFT}
  Dep: {TIME_LOCAL} | Arr: {TIME_LOCAL}
  Price: {AMOUNT} {CURRENCY} ({MYR_EQUIV} MYR)
  Miles Earnable: ~{MILES} {PROGRAM}
  Book: {LINK}

My recommendation: Option {X} because {REASON}.

Please advise and I will proceed with booking.

Best regards,
{PA_NAME}
```

> Always use the program with the highest earn rate for the specific route.

## 🏨 Hotel Research — Best Deal Sources

| Source | Best For | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Booking.com | Widest choice | Often has free cancellation |
| Hotels.com | Loyalty: every 10th night free | Good for repeat stays |
| Marriott / Hyatt direct | Loyalty points | Always check direct rate |
| Secret Escapes | Luxury at discount | [secretescapes.com](https://secretescapes.com) |
| Mr & Mrs Smith | Boutique luxury | [mrandmrssmith.com](https://mrandmrssmith.com) |
| Senior discount codes | Age-specific | Check "senior rate" on hotel direct sites |

**Boss profile for hotel discounts:**
- Age: 67 → ask for senior rates
- Loyalty: mention program membership
- Length of stay: longer stays often qualify for discounts
- Ask: "Do you have a 5th night free or monthly rate available?"
